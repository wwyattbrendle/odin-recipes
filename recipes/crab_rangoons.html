Crab Rangoons

Credit to Grandpa Browns Kitchen for story and recipe.

Description

I was with my Grandfather on a trip to the grocery store top pick up the ingredients for dinner.

I was in the backseat with my feet sticking out the window.

Unfortunately for me it started to rain and sure enough my Grandfather decided to roll up the windows.

I guess I wasn't quick enough because my toe got stuck in the window. I screamed..it hurt like hell. Luckily my grandfather immediately rolled the window back down and my toe was rescued. It was not broken or cut...just bruised.

Grandfather felt really bad. He made it up to me with a very fine dinner. Which included Crab Rangoon which is what we had gone to the grocery store to pick up the ingredients for.

Ingredients

  • 6oz can of crab meat
  • 16oz cream cheese
  • 2 tsp A-1 sauce
  • 1/4 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp hot sauce (optional)
  • 3-5 cups cooking oil (for frying)

Steps

  1. Heat oil in deep fryer or cooking pot.
  2. Crop crab meat into small pieces. Place in bowl.
  3. Add remaining ingredients except oil. Mix well.
  4. Place individual wonton wrappers on flat surface.
  5. Place 1 tsp of mixture in center of each wrapper
  6. Fold, seal and place in hot cooking oil.
  7. Fry 3-5 minutes until folden brown.
  8. Removed cooked wontons from oil and place on paper towel to drain.

Serve warm.
